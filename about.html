About Eternal

Eternal was founded on May 20th, 2024. Starting as a small club with 100 Discord members, we grew exponentially each day to get to where we are today. Eternal now has 15k+ Discord members, 3.3k Twitter followers, 1k YouTube subscribers, and 1k Instagram followers. We do this all for the fans and the community, and we're forever thankful for each one of you.

Our Mission

To build a strong community that empowers every fan to connect, compete, and grow and to deliver exceptional entertainment experiences for our fans through content creation and esports.

Our Vision

To be the most engaging hub for fans to connect, compete, and celebrate the power of entertainment together.
